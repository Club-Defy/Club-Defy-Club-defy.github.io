Fees structure

For each withdrawal, a transfer fee is paid by users to cover the transaction costs of moving the cryptocurrency out of their account.
Withdrawal rates are determined by the blockchain network and can fluctuate without notice due to factors such as network congestion. Please check the most recent data listed on each withdrawal page.
