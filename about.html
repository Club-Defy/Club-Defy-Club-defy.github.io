About Defy

We’re on a mission to change the way you perceive your investments by building a fair, efficient and transparent system that enables you to take charge of your finances.

The world of cryptocurrency is complex and we want you to be able to navigate that with ease; and what better way to do so than along with your friends?

We’re a bunch of chess wizards, cricket WizKids, language connoisseurs and more on an ambitious mission to revolutionise the cryptocurrency space by making it community-first. We love finding elegant solutions to complex problems. We are dreamers and believers, the kind that does not fit into a mould.

But above all, we are empathetic believers, passionate problem solvers. We do not wait for solutions to arrive. We put our heads together, pick up a torchlight and search to find it.
